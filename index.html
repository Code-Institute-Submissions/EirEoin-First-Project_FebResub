Meditation Techniques

Experienced meditators agree: a daily meditation practice can have significant benefits for mental and physical health. But one thing they probably won’t agree on? The most effective types of meditation. That’s simply because it’s different for everyone. After all, there are literally hundreds of meditation techniques encompassing practices from different traditions, cultures, spiritual disciplines, and religions. There’s not a universally accepted “best” or “most effective” type; rather, it is our individual preference that helps us choose the one (or ones) that works best for us. Here are some examples of the different types of techniques ...

  • Mindfulness Meditation
  • Progressive relaxation Meditation
  • Breath Awareness Meditation
  • Kundali Yoga
  • Loving-Kindness Meditation
  • Zen Meditation
